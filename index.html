Fish Care with Martin

It is best to use water, not Coca-Cola, for your fish to swim in

Fish are unskilled in basketball, so avoid putting a hoop in the tank, as this will remind them of their shortcomings

Most fish exhibit no strong religious preferences, so use your own taste for decor

Always be sure your fish are not planning an uprising
